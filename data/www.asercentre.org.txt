## About ASER

ASER Centre uses simple yet rigorous methods to generate evidence at scale on
the impact of social sector programs and policies, focusing mainly on the
education sector.

## Core areas

**Annual Status of Education Report (ASER)** is a nationwide household survey
that captures the status of children’s enrollment and learning outcomes in
rural India every year.  
  
The **Research and Assessment** units design and implement research projects
and measurement tools that delve deeper into different aspects of children’s
educational trajectories.  
  
The **Capacity building** unit helps individuals and institutions understand
the importance and the nuts and bolts of designing simple metrics and
measures.
